my vision

I am a programmer, and also I am a researcher. According to my vision, there is no need to know every functionality or structure of the code. Because the programming is updated almost every day; we get new functionalities, and also new technologies. For learning innovations, we have to search. So I think, the person who wants to be a good programmer has to be a good researcher, too.


And from my perspectives, for succeeding in this life, we should have a vision. If you have a personal vision, you will

  • have a guide for decisions,
  • become meaning-driven and inner-directed,
  • think in the long-term, and
  • maintain balance in your life.
